01

Scope & Application

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") applies to the Proximity Deployment Tracker web application ("Platform") operated by Proximity Lab Services, LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). The Platform is a private, invitation-only business tool used to manage laboratory deployment projects, facility data, compliance tracking, and related operational workflows.

This Policy governs all personal information processed by the Platform, regardless of the device or location from which it is accessed. By accessing or using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

This Policy does not apply to third-party services linked from the Platform, including QuickBooks Online, Bill.com, or Microsoft Azure Active Directory, which have their own privacy policies.

02

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

Account & Identity Information

  • Name and email address (provided by your organization's identity provider via SSO)
  • User role and organizational assignment
  • Profile metadata supplied by Microsoft Azure Active Directory

Platform Usage Data

  • Records you create, edit, or view within the Platform (facility data, milestones, documents, contacts)
  • Activity log entries generated by your actions on the Platform
  • Timestamps of login events and significant actions

Technical & Device Data

  • IP address and browser type (collected automatically for security purposes)
  • Session identifiers and authentication tokens

Communications

  • Support ticket messages and attachments submitted through the Platform
  • System-generated notifications and email confirmations

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Platform operation: Authenticating users, managing access permissions, and providing Platform features
  • Project management: Tracking facility deployments, milestones, compliance requirements, and related data
  • Communication: Sending system notifications, support responses, and operational updates
  • Security & integrity: Detecting, preventing, and responding to unauthorized access or abuse
  • Audit & compliance: Maintaining records for internal auditing and regulatory compliance purposes
  • Service improvement: Understanding how the Platform is used to improve functionality and user experience

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling for commercial purposes, or any purpose inconsistent with the operation of this business Platform.

04

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Supabase (database and authentication infrastructure) and Netlify (hosting) as sub-processors. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.
  • Microsoft Azure AD: Authentication is delegated to your organization's Microsoft identity provider. We receive only the profile information that provider shares with us.
  • Within your organization: Users within your organization may see data you contribute to shared projects within the Platform, consistent with their access role.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of the Company or others.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction, subject to equivalent privacy protections.
05

Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS (HTTPS)
  • Data at rest is encrypted in our Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access data they are authorized to view
  • Authentication is delegated to Microsoft Azure Active Directory, which supports multi-factor authentication
  • Automatic session expiration after periods of inactivity
  • Privileged administrative actions are logged in an immutable audit trail

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a security breach that affects your personal data, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

06

Data Retention

We retain personal information and platform records for as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain active user accounts and fulfill our contractual obligations
  • Comply with applicable legal, tax, and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

When a user account is deactivated, identifying information associated with that account may be anonymized in activity logs rather than deleted, to preserve the integrity of project records. Upon written request, we will discuss retention practices specific to your account.

08

Single Sign-On (SSO)

The Platform uses Microsoft Azure Active Directory as the exclusive authentication provider. You cannot create a standalone Platform account with a username and password separate from your organization's identity provider.

When you authenticate via SSO, Microsoft provides us with your name, email address, and profile metadata. We do not receive or store your Microsoft password. Your organization controls which users are granted access to the Platform, and revoking access at the identity provider level will immediately prevent Platform access.

Important: Your use of Microsoft Azure AD is governed by Microsoft's Privacy Policy and your organization's policies. Proximity Lab Services is not responsible for data processing performed by Microsoft as part of the authentication process.

09

Cookies & Tracking

The Platform uses the following types of storage mechanisms:

  • Session tokens: Stored in your browser's local storage to maintain your authenticated session. These expire automatically and are required for Platform operation.
  • Preferences: We may store non-identifying user preferences (such as sidebar state or selected organization) in your browser's local storage to improve usability.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, analytics tracking pixels, or any cross-site tracking technologies. We do not use Google Analytics or similar consumer analytics services on the Platform.
