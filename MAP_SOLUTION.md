## How It Works

The map uses:
1. **Leaflet** for the mapping engine
2. **OpenStreetMap tiles** for the map imagery (completely free)
3. **Custom markers** with color-coding based on facility status
4. **Interactive popups** showing facility information
5. **Full integration** with your existing facility data
